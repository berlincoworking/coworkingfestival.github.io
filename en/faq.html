FAQ for Visitors

What is coworking?

Coworking is about collaborating with the people around you, whether or not they’re in the same field or company. The aim is to provide a professional and social network without depending on a specific employer or company, and gaining more tools for flexibility and creative output. In short—it’s the new way to work.

How much does it cost to participate in the festival?

It is free of charge to try out the spaces! This also applies to most events that are listed in our schedule, if not you see a hint.

Which Coworking Spaces are participating in the Coworking Festival?

Our homepage has a list with photos and links to each of the spaces that are participating.

Do I need to register somewhere?

Registration for the Coworking Festival is optional. Some spaces will require a registration for trial days or certain events. Details can be found on the space profile on this website.

FAQ for State Ambassadors

What is a State Ambassador?

You are an important liaison between the festival organizers, participating coworking spaces, sponsors, and stakeholders in your federal state.

What are the responsibilities of a State Ambassador?

  • Reaching out to and coordinating with coworking spaces
  • Organizing a flagship event (bonus)
  • Press and communication
  • Collaborating with other ambassadors
  • Finding funding or sponsorship (bonus)

Which states are you still looking for a State Ambassador?

We are still looking for ambassadors for Bremen, Hamburg, Rheinland-Pfalz, Sachsen, and Saarland. If you are interested, contact us.

My coworking space is hosting an event for the festival, how can I share it?

We would be happy to share your event in our event calendar and on our social media channels. The requirement is that you have registered your space for the festival. We will contact you in due course after registration to get details about your event.

What are the benefits of participating?

  • Your own space profile on the Coworking Festival website, including a link and a list of your events.
  • Social media exposure on LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook through the promotion of your space and events.
  • Access to the public sector.
  • Press kit.
  • Networking opportunities to increase your reach.
