Free Coworking Tour Berlin

Wednesday, September 7 2022 – 10:00am

A tour with Matthias to explore the different coworking spaces in Berlin that are part of the Coworking Festival event.
Let's get inspired about how different operators and locations implement coworking.
This is a semi-self guided experience using public transport. It will go on for the whole day or until we get tired, but you can drop out the tour.
Meeting point is the U6 subway station "Seestrasse" in Wedding in front of the Cafe Chokkolatta at 10am. If you arrive earlier, have a coffee there.
Matthias will have an orange Coworking Bansko umbrella.
This is a free event but you will have to buy your own tickets for transport.

Free Coworking Tour Brandenburg

Thursday, September 8 2022 – 10:17-18:40 Uhr

Our tour will start in Bad Belzig, where a new coworking space is being built in the train station and where the creative places "Sanderberger" and "Zukunftsschusterei" are located in the old town. Than we take the train to Wiesenburg/Mark. At Gleis 21 coworking, there is not only good WiFi for working purposes, but also nice people who give you an idea on working at the station and the future KoDorf plans. One result of creative work in our regin is the art trail. It starts right in front of Gleis 21. On a 1-1.5 hour walk you will experience a selection of artworks, the landscape park of Wiesenburg/Mark Castle and pure nature. And we share a glimpse of the next art project - an extension of the existing path with five AR artworks. We continue by bus to Klein Glien to visit Coconat - THE workation place in the region. Here you will get to know the wide range of workation offers from the Makerspace, to coworking in the S-Bahn wagon (what?!:-)), etc. Relax before you take the bus and train back home.
